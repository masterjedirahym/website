    About Us

    We are young professionals engaging in the complex process of joining the entry-level workforce. As more qualified graduates seek employment , there is a greater need to engage them in a constructive way.

    Our Mission

    Stubble was founded with the goal of connecting people through effective communication. To us, networking and communication foster prosperity in any context. We seek to mend the disconnect between companies and acquiring quality talent. Stubble can bridge the gap.

    Our Philosophy

    We engage the young professional crowd with an integrated approach that sets us apart from anyone else. We are on a quest to help people through reforming the talent aquisition process. The future of your company is built upon a calculated investment in hard workers, team players, and holistic problem-solvers.

    Your Vision

    You have to win the day, everyday. Your company needs the tools, team, and knowledge to achieve its goals. You are pushed forth by the people who stand behind you. Our people are your people. Let us enhance your workflow dynamic and help you execute your vision.

    Services

    We are offering unparalleled access to a variety of qualified entry-level tech candidates who are eager for work, willing to learn, and will improve the culture and workflow in any environment in which they are placed. Our recruiters are integrated into the college population and have unmatched access to quality candidates. If you are interested in gaining more access to fresh talent, reach out today.

    People

    We invest in every candidate we put forth. We screen, interview, and assess them for technical proficiency, work ethic, and academic success. Through this process, we work to ensure that they emulate the qualities of a model employee.

    Planet

    We strive to increase opportunities for bright young professionals by connecting them with globally competitive, diverse, and exceptional organizations. Join us in creating more opportunities for aspiring professionals across the country.

    Profit

    Your customer base and profit stream are vital to the success of your company and the livelihood of your employees. Our candidates have the tools necessary to make your organization stand out, from the ground up.

    Our Skills

    Our candidates and recruiters have gained experience with a large variety of technologies through academia, projects, internships, and jobs. We vouch for team-players, complex problem-solvers, and ingenuity. With whatever opportunities you bring to us, we can assure you that we will provide individuals who are willing to learn, perform, and maintain a respectable reputation as a professional.
